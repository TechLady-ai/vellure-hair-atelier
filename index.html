FAQ

How long does shipping take?

Processing is typically 1–3 business days. Once your order ships, delivery usually takes 2–5 business days for U.S. orders depending on the shipping method selected at checkout.

Is this real RAW hair?

Our RAW collections are sourced from trusted suppliers and are cuticle-aligned, unprocessed and built to last 2–5 years with proper care.

What is HD Melt Lace?

HD Melt Lace is ultra-thin, soft lace designed to blend seamlessly into the skin for a nearly invisible hairline. It is perfect for beginners and stylists who want a flawless melt.

Do you accept returns?

For hygiene reasons, hair must be unused, uninstalled, and in original packaging to be eligible for return. Please create a detailed return policy in your Shopify store that reflects your exact terms.
